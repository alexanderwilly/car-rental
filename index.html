Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, simply login to our website and select the location, date, and time you would like to pick up and return the vehicle. You can choose the vehicle that best suits your needs and complete the reservation process online. If you have any questions or need assistance, our customer support team is available to help you.

What types of vehicles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of vehicles to suit your needs, including cars and SUVs. Our fleet includes vehicles from top manufacturers which are well-maintained and reliable. Whether you need a compact car for a quick trip around town or a spacious SUV for a family vacation, we have the perfect vehicle for you.

What are your rates?

Our rates vary depending on the type of vehicle you choose and the duration of the rental. We offer competitive rates on all our vehicles to help you save money on your next trip. You can view our rates and make a reservation online or contact our customer support team for more information.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes, our rental vehicles come with insurance coverage to protect you in the event of an accident or damage to the vehicle. Our insurances include liability coverage, collision damage waiver, and personal accident insurance. These insurances are included in the rental rate, so you can drive with peace of mind knowing you are protected.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation options for all reservations, allowing you to cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pick-up time. For more information on our cancellation policy, please contact our customer support team.
