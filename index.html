Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, simply login to our website and select the location, date, and time you would like to pick up and return the vehicle. You can then choose the vehicle that best suits your needs and complete the reservation process online. If you have any questions or need assistance, our customer support team is available to help you.

What types of vehicles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of vehicles to suit your needs, including cars and SUVs. Our fleet includes vehicles from top manufacturers which are well-maintained and reliable. Whether you need a compact car for a quick trip around town or a spacious SUV for a family vacation, we have the perfect vehicle for you.

What are your rates?

Our rates vary depending on the type of vehicle you choose and the duration of the rental. We offer competitive rates on all our vehicles to help you save money on your next trip. You can view our rates and make a reservation online or contact our customer support team for more information.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes, we offer insurance options for all our rental vehicles to provide you with peace of mind during your rental. Our insurance options include liability coverage, collision damage waiver, and personal accident insurance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of reservation you have made. We offer flexible cancellation options for most reservations, allowing you to cancel or modify your reservation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pick-up time. For more information on our cancellation policy, please contact our customer support team.
